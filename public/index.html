How it works

A control plane you own, reconciling a gateway you run.

1 · Configure

Define orgs, projects, providers, budgets, and guardrails in the console. One source of truth — no hand-edited YAML.

2 · Reconcile

The control plane applies your config to the gateway automatically — consistent, auditable, and free of drift.

3 · Govern

Every request is authenticated, budgeted, guarded, and observed — with per-org dashboards and a full audit log.
